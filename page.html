It was the time that I was able to acknowledge the importance of overcoming my limits. Approximately, we rode 320 km within 3 days and had a chance to encounter certain dangers such as being isolated in the mountains when the light was fading under the darkness. Likewise, a threat from the wild animals had frightened us in the darkness as well. However, I still remember the emotion when I smelled a breeze of sea coming through my nose which had a victorious signification that it is over! It was the moment and memory that I would never exchange with something else but I will always cherish all the memories I have made. Furthermore, I would like to thank my bros who had a chance to share such a great moment of accomplishment with me.

I started to feel that I wanted experience more adventures after the bike trip with my bros. So, I have begun to challenge myself by climbing the tallest mountain in Kansai and in Shiga. The way to the top of the mountain was tough and it required a lot of stamina and endurance. However, once I reached to the top, I was able to forget all of my pain and it was the time that I could feel my effort and motivation. "I will never give up".
